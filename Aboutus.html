Los Tres Amigos taqueria is a small family restaurant founded by three friends. Together, the "chicken brothers" made a name for themselves and their small-town eatery by using authentic flavors and spices to make the tastiest chicken north of the border. Since then, Los Tres Amigos have been serving families just like yours. And we look forward to continued growth!
